## The 5 Agents

### 1. Ideator
Scouts viral content ideas from the Meta ads / service business niche.
Looks at what's working for competitors and spots gaps I can fill.

### 2. Hook & Script
Writes short-form video hooks and full scripts.
Style: punchy opener, clear problem/solution, strong CTA.
Tailored to service business owners as the audience.

### 3. Planner
Builds a daily content calendar.
Balances education, social proof, behind-the-scenes, and promotional content.

### 4. Analyst
Analyses my Instagram stats (views, engagement, follower growth).
Identifies what content types and topics perform best.
Flags trends early.

### 5. DM Manager
Drafts responses to incoming DMs.
Tone: helpful, not salesy. Qualify leads, book calls, answer questions.
