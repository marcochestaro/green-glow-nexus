The Philosophy
Value first. Pitch rarely. Trust compounds.
Post content so genuinely useful that service business owners think "this person actually knows what they're doing — I want them running my ads." That trust builds post by post. Pitch once every 5–7 posts. When you do, it lands because they already believe in you.
The 5 Pillars
1

The Breakdown

Visual explainers, Miro board walkthroughs. Gets saves and shares. Positions you as the person who actually understands this.

2

Industry Truths

Hot takes and real opinions. Things business owners are already thinking but haven't heard clearly. Gets comments, shares, follows.

3

Behind the Scenes

Screen records, process walkthroughs. The most credibility-building content you can make. Shows you do the work, not just talk.

4

Quick Wins

One tip per video, under 60 seconds. "Follow for one of these every Friday" converts viewers to followers on a schedule.

5

Personal & Presence

No agenda. Your story, what you're building. What makes someone go from casual viewer to actually rooting for you.
